About

Sheridan Duma is a 26-year-old American photographer currently residing in the breathtaking landscapes of France. At the age of 22, shetook a leap of faith and followed her dreams to this enchanting country, and it has been an incredible journey ever since.

While photography has always held a special place in her heart, her path led her to become and English teacher, allowing her to share her love for language and culture with others. Living in Europe has provided her with an abundance of opportunities to explore and capture fascinating moments through her lens.

Join her on this extraordinary adventure as we explore the enchanting landscapes, unravel the cultural tapestries, and discover the hidden treasures that Europe has to offer. Together, let us celebrate the beauty that lies within every frame and find inspiration in the vast possibilities that await.

Thank you for visiting her website, and she hopes her photographs transport you to a world of wonde and evoke the same joy and curiosity in you that she experiences behind the camera.

Stay tuned for more captivating visuals as she continues to embark on thie lifelong pursuit of capturing the world's beauty one click at a time.
